Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

  • How do I book a car? You can easily book a car online through our website by visiting the 'Services' section.
  • What do I need to rent a car? You will need a valid driver’s license, proof of identity, and a credit card for payment.
  • How do I extend my rental period? Please contact our customer support team via phone or email to extend your rental period.
  • What if I have an accident? In case of an accident, please contact our support team immediately. We will guide you through the process and help you with the necessary steps.
